Tummy Butter for Stretch Marks

WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT

Our #1 best-selling Tummy Butter for Stretch Marks helps prevent those tell-tale scars so you can transform from striped zebra to radiant lioness!

A pure cocoa butter base is enhanced with 11 nourishing oils that include organic rosehip, tamanu & sea buckthorn. Rich in vitamins A & E and essential fatty acids, the belly butter helps promote collagen and increase cell regeneration for firmer, more youthful skin.

With its lush orange-chocolate scent, Tummy Butter is the most effective way to help prevent stretch marks during pregnancy. It’s so good, you’ll swear it contained unicorn tears!*

*No unicorns were harmed in the making of this product.

Sizes available: 4 oz, 8 oz

Why It Kicks Butt
  • helps prevent new stretch marks
  • minimizes appearance of new stretch marks
  • helps face C-section scars
  • soothes itchy, stressed skin
  • soothes sunburns & bug bites

Safe for:

  • Pregnancy
  • Breastfeeding
  • Postpartum
How To Use

Massage Tummy Butter lotion onto body twice a day, or as needed. Concentrate on areas that need extra lovin’ as baby continues to grow. For maximum stretch mark & prevention, use with  Bump Gloss pregnancy stretch mark oil.

Tips:

  • Tummy Butters are most beneficial in preventing & fading new stretch marks.
  • Always belly butter in a circular motion to promote blood flow.
  • Avoid nipple area while breastfeeding.
  • Tummy butters are most beneficial on new stretch marks.
  • Consult your doctor to ensure you’re getting the essential prenatal vitamins needed for healthy baby, body & skin!

Pregnancy Safe Ingredients

  • Cocoa Butter

    A pure butter from the cacao bean that's rich in antioxidants and skin-protecting properties. Natural emollient for dry, stressed skin.

  • Organic Tamanu Oil

    Ancient Polynesian skin care treatment from the tamanu fruit. A well-researched anti-inflammatory that aids in diminishing pregnancy stretch marks, scarring, and rashes.

  •  Organic Sea Buckthorn Oil

    Powerful oil prized for its ability to recover skin damage caused by pregnancy stretch marks, wounds, and burns. Loaded with carotenoids and vitamins A, C, and E.

  •  Organic Rosehip Oil

    Non-greasy oil that fights dehydrated, damaged and aging skin. Gentle & full of vitamins, rosehip oil has been coveted by ancient Egyptians, Native Americans, and Mayans.

Full Ingredients:

Aqua (Distilled Water), Theobroma Cacao (Cocoa) Seed Butter, Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter, Aloe Barbadensis (Aloe Vera) Leaf Juice, Prunus Amygdalus Dulcis (Sweet Almond) Oil, Persea Americana (Avocado) Oil*, Rosa Canina (Rosehip) Fruit Oil*, Calophyllum Inophyllum (Tamanu) Seed Oil*, Aleurites Moluccana (Kukui) Seed Oil*, Hippophae Rhamnoides (Sea Buckthorn) Oil*, Limnanthes Alba (Meadowfoam) Seed Oil, Olea Europaea (Olive) Fruit Oil, Vitamin E (Mixed Tocopherols from GMO-Free Sources), Vegetable Glycerin USP, Emulsifying Wax NF, Sodium Hydroxymethglycinate, Phenoxyethanol, Caprylyl Glycol and Essential Oils*
