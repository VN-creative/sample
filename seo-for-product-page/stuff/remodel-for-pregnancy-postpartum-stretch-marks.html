How To Use

On clean skin, once you notice stretch marks dab a pea-sized amount on stretch marks. Allow drying completely before applying a stretch mark prevention cream, moisturizer or getting dressed. For maximum stretch mark prevention, follow with The Spoiled Mama’s Belly Butter for Stretch Marks.

Tips:

  • The serum may increase skin’s sensitivity to the sun, particularly the possibility of sunburn. Use sunscreen and limit sun exposure while using this product, and for a week after.
  • The pregnancy stretch marks removal serum is most effective on new stretch marks. 
  • While stretch marks are new, stay out of sun and tanning beds.
  • Help your skin heal by staying hydrated and eating a well-balanced diet with adequate amounts of vitamin E and C.
  • The best way to prevent stretch marks during pregnancy is early treatment with serums, creams, and oils.

*The FDA approves retinol (vitamin A) during pregnancy with daily limits of 1.5 mg. This serum contains 0.5% retinol and does not exceed the FDA’s recommendation when applied as a spot treatment. Please discuss with your doctor or OBGYN about which treatment will work best for you. 

Pregnancy Safe Ingredients

  • Witch Hazel

    Beloved for its antibacterial and antiseptic qualities, it helps tighten and refresh skin. Promotes faster wound healing and soothes distressed skin.

  • Jojoba Oil

    Gentle, natural liquid wax produced from the jojoba shrub. Absorbs quickly, and helps soften skin. Rich in vitamin B and minerals like copper and zinc.

  • Green Tea

    Powerful antioxidant and anti-inflammatory extracted from green tea leaves. Helps relieve redness and irritation in skin, and may improve the the appearance of aging skin.

  • Aloe Vera

    Renowned for its medicinal properties, this cactus is an excellent treatment for distressed skin, burns and minor wounds. Naturally rich in vitamins A and E, magnesium and zinc.
