How To Use

After your daily shower, allow Bump Gloss belly oil to lock in your skin’s moisture by applying on slightly damp skin. In the afternoon or evening, massage belly butter onto your body and allow to dry before dressing. Increase usage as needed and as your belly continues to grow!

Tips:

  • You can start this regime as early as 2 - 12 weeks into your pregnancy!
  • Always apply stretch mark treatments in a gentle circular motion to increase blood flow.
  • Apply stretch mark oil outside bathtub/shower, as it can make shower floor slippery.
  • Create a regular belly moisturizing routine to ensure healthy skin.
  • Discuss your diet with your doctor to make sure you’re feeding your skin from the inside out!

What results have you found with Tummy Butter & Bump Gloss?
In random surveys of users, 92% of pregnant women using Spoiled Mama's Tummy Butter for stretch marks and Bump Gloss pregnancy oil, reported they did not get one lasting stretch mark and claimed our belly butter & belly oil was the only solution to their excessive itching.

82% of Tummy Butter users that treated already existing pregnancy stretch marks experienced a significant improvement in an average of 8 weeks when used twice daily. The results included fading, blending and smoothing of existing scars and stretch marks by improving skin tone and texture.
